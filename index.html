Real-Time Business Scenario

The Business Problem

SpicePot is a multi-cuisine restaurant chain with 25 outlets across South India offering dine-in, takeaway, and home delivery.

The operations head has observed that weekend peak hours are causing long waiting times, certain menu items are consistently underperforming, and delivery orders are increasing customer complaints due to cold food and long delivery times.

IBM Cognos Analytics is being used to analyze sales, menu performance, and service quality.

Weekend peak hours causing customer dissatisfaction

Delivery Complaints

Case Study Questions

Six Analytical Challenges

Q1
Reporting

Menu Performance Report

Create a Menu Performance Report showing Menu Item, Category, Total Quantity Ordered, Total Revenue, and Contribution % to Total Revenue.

🎨 Conditional Styling: Items contributing < 1% in Red (consider removing)
🔽 Prompts for Outlet City and Category
📄 Header with SpicePot branding, Footer with page number
🔤 Font: Palatino Linotype, size 11
Q2
Reporting

Delivery Performance Report

Build a Delivery Performance Report listing all delivery orders with Order Time, Preparation Time, Delivery Time, Total Time, and Customer Rating.

🧮 Calculated field: Total Delivery Time = Preparation + Delivery
🎨 Styling: >60 mins → Red, 45–60 → Amber, <45 → Green
📊 Summary: Average delivery time by Outlet City in footer
Q3
Reporting

Hourly Order Volume Trends

Using Report Visualize Prompt, allow Outlet Managers to select chart type (Bar or Line) to view Hourly Order Volume Trends across different days of the week.

🔽 Prompts for Outlet Name and Order Type
📝 Summarize peak hour, busiest day, most popular category as text objects
🎨 Conditional Styling: hours with Cancelled orders >10 in Amber
Q4
Exploration

Cognos Exploration – Pattern Discovery

Use Cognos Exploration to discover patterns in the SpicePot dataset exploring relationship between Order Type and Customer Rating.

🔍 Investigate which Feedback Tags appear most frequently for Delivery orders
📈 Correlate Feedback Tags with Delivery Time
🏆 Identify top 5 best-performing menu items across all outlets
Q5
Dashboard

SpicePot Operations Dashboard

Design a comprehensive SpicePot Operations Dashboard with multiple visualizations and KPI tiles.

📊 KPI Tiles: Total Revenue, Avg Customer Rating, Order Cancellation Rate, Avg Delivery Time
📊 Bar chart: Revenue by Menu Category
📈 Line chart: Daily Revenue Trend
🥧 Pie chart: Order Type Distribution (Dine-In/Takeaway/Delivery)
📋 Waiter Performance table by Rating
🔽 Add Outlet and Date filters
Q6
Story Making

Monthly Operations Review Story

Create a Cognos Story for SpicePot's Monthly Operations Review with 6 narrative scenes.

1️⃣ Scene 1: Chain-wide Sales & Order Overview
2️⃣ Scene 2: Top & Bottom Performing Menu Items
3️⃣ Scene 3: Delivery Performance & Complaint Analysis
4️⃣ Scene 4: Customer Satisfaction by Outlet & Order Type
5️⃣ Scene 5: Peak Hour & Demand Pattern Analysis
6️⃣ Scene 6: Menu Optimization & Delivery Improvement Recommendations
